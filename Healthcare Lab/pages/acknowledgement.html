Official Acknowledgments and Medical Advisory Board Recognition

The establishment and continuous development of this digital cognitive health platform are made possible through the invaluable expertise, strategic oversight, and scholarly contributions of our distinguished Medical Advisory Board and senior consulting specialists. We wish to express our profound gratitude to Dr. Arthur Vance, MD, PhD, whose exceptional clinical leadership and lifelong dedication to neurodegenerative medicine have provided the foundational framework for our signature cognitive longevity protocols. His vision remains instrumental in anchoring our platform’s medical standards. Furthermore, we extend our formal appreciation to Dr. Ethan Vance and Dr. Maya Lin, whose specialized clinical insights in neurology and neuropsychology have meticulously shaped our interactive brain-function baselines, diagnostic assessments, and cognitive rehabilitation exercises.


In addition to our foundational clinical frameworks, our patient-facing supportive resources have been significantly enriched by the contributions of Dr. Kenji Sato and Dr. Claire Sterling. Their extensive experience in geriatric psychiatry and behavioral therapy enabled the creation of our mental wellness workflows, digital stress-reduction blueprints, and specialized toolkits dedicated to caregiver support and family transitions. We also honor Dr. Naomi Brooks and Dr. Chloe Nguyen for their vital work in bridging the gap between complex neuroplasticity research and the practical application of integrative, holistic lifestyle medicine into sustainable daily habits. Through the collective dedication, academic rigor, and public health commitment of these medical professionals, this platform is privileged to deliver evidence-based, science-backed resources designed to empower our global visitors to proactively navigate, preserve, and optimize their long-term neurological well-being.
