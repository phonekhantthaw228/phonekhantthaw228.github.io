Year Title Description University/ Granting Body Criteria for Wining
2003 ARCS Graduate Fellowship Financial grant and recognition for high-achieving doctoral students in science and engineering. ARCS Foundation (San Francisco Bay Area) Awarded for demonstrating exceptional scientific potential and achievement during his Ph.D. research.
2005 Allan G. Marr Prize An annual academic honor awarded for outstanding doctoral work. University of California, Davis Awarded specifically for submitting the Best Ph.D. Dissertation in neuroscience that year.

2017 Cogan Award A premier international honor recognizing significant, pioneering contributions to vision science. Association for Research in Vision and Ophthalmology (ARVO) Awarded for making the most significant discoveries in the study of vision and ophthalmology.
